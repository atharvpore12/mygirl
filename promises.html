🤞 Promises to You, Samaa

These are the words I’ll live by — for us, forever 💛

1. I promise to always love you

No matter the distance, the mood, or the day — my love will stay constant, unshaken, and golden.

2. I promise to listen, really listen

Even in silence, I will hear your heart. Your words, your feelings — they’ll always matter to me.

3. I promise to hold on in every storm

Fights, misunderstandings, distance — nothing will make me let go of you.

4. I promise to make you laugh

Even on your worst days, I’ll be the reason a small smile shows up unexpectedly 😊

5. I promise to grow with you

We’ll learn, mess up, and evolve — together, stronger every day 💪

6. I promise to never stop choosing you

Over and over again, through every phase of life — it’ll always be you, Samaa 💖
